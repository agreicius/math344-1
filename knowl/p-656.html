Let \(Y\) be a subset of the topological space \(X\text{.}\) An open covering of \(Y\) is a collection of open sets \(\mathcal{U}=\{U_i\}_{i\in I}\) such that \(Y\subseteq \bigcup_{i\in I} U_i\text{.}\) As a shorthand, we will often simply say in this case that \(Y=\subseteq_{i\in I}U_i\) is an open covering.
